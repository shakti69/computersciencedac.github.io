About The Department

Pioneering Academic Excellence in Computation Since 1995

Department History

The Department of Computer Science at Dhenkanal Autonomous College was established in the year 1995 with the vision of importing quality education in the field of information technology to the students of this historical region.

Over the decades, the department has grown significantly, fostering a culture of innovation, rigorous technical training, and ethical leadership. Our alumni are currently placed in reputed MNCs across the globe, contributing critically to the digital domain.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer science education and research, empowering learners with cutting-edge technological competencies suited to solve real-world problems and enhance the socioeconomic fabric of society.

Our Mission

  • Deliver structured and rigorous curricula in alignment with global IT standards.
  • Foster an environment of innovation, practical application, and independent coding.
  • Promote moral and ethical values alongside technical skills.
  • Encourage industry-academia collaboration for continuous skill enhancement.
